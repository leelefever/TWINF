Reviews of Mervyn Perera, Driver and Guide in Sri Lanka

As you may have seen, we worked with Mervyn in Sri Lanka and became friends. Since that time, this website has helped his business and sometimes grateful customers of his send us reviews of his services.

Note: Although we worked with Mervyn in 2006, he is still working as a guide and driver as of 2013 and has recently expanded his services. Mervyn can be reached by email: pereramervyn2005.mp@gmail.com

The reviews are below:

Trip to Sri Lanka - The Neyts family, Leuven, Belgium

We enjoyed very much our vacation in Sri Lanka that was perfectly organized by Mervyn Perera. The planning of our vacation together with Mervyn went very smoothly. To any of the many questions that we had, we received very rapidly feedback, often within hours (even on New Yearsday). Mervyn and driver Alex picked us up at Colombo airport. We then enjoyed a few hours of sleep and some pleasant time at the pool and beach in a very comfortable hotel in Negombo. The two week tour in Sri Lanka was truly amazing. A country with so many interesting aspects. The selection of hotels was price-quality wise excellent. Here and there we wanted/had to make small changes to our plans, Alex was always very flexible. Most of all, he is a very safe driver, which is really important in the hectic traffic of Sri Lanka. Mervyn is apparently now working with 7 drivers, others informed us that also other the other drivers drive very safely. The Toyota-van was spacious, comfortable and clean (small minor point, no seatbelts for the passangers in our van). We can very much recommend a vacation/trip in Sri Lanka organized by Mervyn Perera.

All in all we can highly recommend choosing Mervin Perera with his knowledge and experience of choosing the right driver, hotels and tour program for you!

The review below is from Jane and Bill Stymest

When planning our trip to Sri Lanka in November 2011, I came across your website and noted the name and e-mail of Mervyn Perera. We were looking for a driver and it was good to find one so well recommended. The recommendations were well justified. I began e-mailing Mervyn in November for our forthcoming trip in February 2012 hoping we could book him for our driver. Unfortunately, at that time, he had another tour booked but said he could place us with a very reliable driver, Alex. We agreed and various e-mails went back and forth with regard to ideas for the tour, suggested stopover times, anticipated temperatures in the hill country etc. Mervyn always came straight back with helpful suggestions and he played an integral part in helping us plan our holiday.

We arrived in Sri Lanka and stayed the first night in Negombo and how delighted we were when Mervyn arrived to pick us up. Apparently, he had been able to reschedule tours and was able to fit us in for our seven days aqfter all. We had independently booked hotels in Sigyria and Ella but had asked Mervyn to recommend a hotel in Kandy as well as our final destination on the South Coast. Retrospectively, I wished I had asked him to book all the hotels as his choices were definitely better (and cheaper) than the two we booked. During our e-mail exchange I had asked him whether he would recommend Unawatuna or Tangalle for our "rest up" by the sea and I am so delighted we took his advice. He informed us that Unawatuna had many tourists but could book us into a cabana on a quiet beach in Tangalle. It was a beautiful beach. We could walk out of our cabana, onto the beach and then walk 20 minutes in either direction along the beach without meeting another tourist. We could also wake up in the morning and wander down to the beach to watch local fishermen hauling in their catch from the shore, or small groups of children walking to school, using the beach as a shortcut.

On arrival at Tangalle, we had originally planned to say goodby to Mervyn but a final discussion where I was seeking his advice about journey time between Tangalle and our last two night stopover in Bentotal, made us realise we had made a rather silly mistake on journey expectation time between Bentota and the airport. Mervyn told us we would, in fact, have to leave our hotel around 4.30 a.m. and anticipate a 4-5 hour drive to the airport, allowing extra time to travel through Colombo during rush hour. It seemed hardly worthwhile stopping over at Bentota for two nights. As a solution, Mervyn suggested we could stay another night at Tangalle (which really appealed) and he would drive back down for us in five days time (a 7-8 hour drive for him) arriving the night before we left and we could then use the following day to tour the coast road, stopping off at Galle and any other place of interest on route. He would then book a hotel for us in Negombo and return the next morning to take us to the airport at 7.30 a.m. This seemed much more sensible and would also save us some money as independent taxis would have been more than the rate Mervyn offered.

As a person, Mervyn is quiet, very courteous, extremely knowledgeable and very helpful. As a driver, he is outstanding. In a country, where the majority of drivers (especially of local buses and three wheelers) have a death wish, I never felt unsafe with him and I don't think my right foot pressed an imaginary brake pedal once during the many, many miles we travelled with him. At various tourist sites, he had his own favourite guides and would phone ahead so they would be waiting for us. They cost very little and their knowledge and English was excellent. We climbed Sigiyria rock following heavy rain and the steps were like a waterfall. Our young guide insisted on carrying my backpack all the way and looked out for my safety.

So.......... if there are people out there looking for a driver, my husband and I can thoroughly recommend Mervyn and are happy for my e-mail to be passed on if others want more information.

Kind Regards,
Jane and Bill Stymest
