San Francisco Airport

By: leelefever on October 8, 2005 - 10:19am
As I mentioned before, I have a history of being late and lost in the San Francisco area. This trend has been reversed starting today. I left Sonoma and drove directly to the SF airport without a single problem. AND, I'm early! Right now I'm typing away on the Treo sitting in the airport thinking about how stupid it is that it costs $10 for wifi access in the airport when it's free in some whole neighborhoods in Seattle. I just went over the Golden Gate Bridge - you can see a running total of my pictures here: http://www.flickr.com/photos/leelefever.
