About This Site

Our Travels, Your Experiences

2006 was a year in which we travelled around the world via 29 countries.  Our trip lasted one year, starting in December, 2005. Our first international destination was New Zealand and our last was Spain.

This site is frozen - we stopped blogging here on January 6th, 2006.  If you'd like to keep up with us, check out Lee LeFever dot com (a personal blog) or Common Craft for our consulting company. 

The information below will help you learn more this site.

To get the most out of TwinF, notice that two basic things happened here:

  1. We blogged the trip. The front page of the site has "Our Dispatches" which are entries we write about the trip.  We are the only authors of the dispatches and they are browsable via Global Map and Keywords.
  2. We asked members to share their experiences. We call these "Member Travel Experiences" and they are entries written by the members and organized by Country Pages. Our hope is that these first-hand experiences will help us (and others) have a better trip.
